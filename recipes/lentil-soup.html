Lentil Soup

Lentil soup is a great dish that can be eaten any time, any season. It is one of the most likes soups.

This recipe will guide you on how to make a lentil soup.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons of sunflower seed oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tablespoon of flour
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 potato
  • 1 desertspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1,5 water glass of red lentils
  • 6 water glasses of water

For the top

  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 dessertspoon of red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Add 3 tablespoons of oil to a deep pan. Fry 1 large chopped onion with vegetable oil.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of flour to the roasted onions and continue the roasting process until they gain smell and color. Add a carrot and a potato, which you cut into large pieces, into the pot and continue mixing.
  3. Add salt, black pepper, 1,5 water glass of lentils and mix well.
  4. Add 6 water glasses of water.
  5. Close lid, cook for 40 minutes until the potatos and carrots have softened while stirring from time to time.
  6. For a great texture, use a stick blender. Remove from the oven after 5 minutes more of cooking.
  7. Heat 3 tablespoons of oil and 2 tablespoons of butter in a pan. Add 1 teaspoon of powdered red pepper on it and after heating the oil for 2 minutes, take it off the stove.
  8. Take the soup in a bowl and pour the oil you have heated on it and serve.
